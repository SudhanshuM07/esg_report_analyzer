GreenCorp Industries Ltd. — ESG Sustainability Report 2024

EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

GreenCorp Industries is committed to sustainable business practices across all operations. 
This report covers our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance for the 
fiscal year 2024, aligned with GRI Standards, TCFD recommendations, and UN SDGs.

---

ENVIRONMENTAL PERFORMANCE

Carbon Emissions & Climate
GreenCorp reduced total Scope 1 and Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions by 18% compared 
to FY2023, reaching 45,230 tCO2e. This was achieved through investment in energy 
efficiency upgrades at our manufacturing plants and a transition to renewable energy 
procurement via Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs).

Renewable Energy: Renewable energy now accounts for 42% of our total energy consumption 
(up from 28% in 2023). We are on track to reach 60% by 2026.

Energy Intensity: Our energy intensity improved to 12.4 GJ per unit produced, a 9% 
improvement from the prior year.

Water Management: Total water withdrawal decreased by 12%. Water recycling rate reached 
68% across all facilities. Zero liquid discharge achieved at our flagship plant.

Waste: We achieved zero-waste-to-landfill at 6 out of 8 manufacturing facilities. 
Total waste recycled or recovered: 94.2%.

Biodiversity: We completed environmental impact assessments for all new projects 
near biodiversity-sensitive areas.

---

SOCIAL PERFORMANCE

Employee Health & Safety
Lost Time Injury Rate (LTIR) fell to 0.42 per 100,000 hours worked — a 23% improvement. 
We launched a new behavioral safety program in Q2 2024 across all sites.

Workforce Diversity & Inclusion
Total employees: 12,400 worldwide.
Women in workforce: 41%
Women in leadership roles: 34% (up from 28% in 2023)
Employees from minority backgrounds in management: 22%
Pay equity ratio (women vs men): 0.97 (near parity)

Training & Development
Average training hours per employee: 38 hours/year.
₹ 4.2 crore invested in employee upskilling and digital training programs.
95% of employees completed mandatory ethics and anti-harassment training.

Community Engagement
Our community investment programs reached 50,000+ beneficiaries across 12 regions.
Total community spend: ₹ 8.5 crore.
Focused areas: education scholarships, clean water access, rural livelihood programs.

Supply Chain
75% of key Tier-1 suppliers completed our ESG questionnaire (up from 52%).
12 supplier audits conducted for labor and environmental standards.

GOVERNANCE PERFORMANCE

Board Composition
Total board members: 12
Independent directors: 9 (75%) — exceeds recommended 60% threshold
Women on board: 4 (33%)
Board ESG committee established in 2024 with quarterly review cadence

Ethics & Compliance
Zero confirmed cases of bribery or corruption.
Anti-corruption training completed by 98% of employees.
Whistleblower hotline received 14 reports — all investigated and resolved.

Executive Compensation
CEO pay ratio to median employee: 42:1
ESG KPIs now account for 20% of executive variable pay.
Long-term incentive plans linked to net-zero progress.

Data Privacy & Cybersecurity
Zero material data breaches in FY2024.
ISO 27001 certification achieved at 3 data centers.
Third-party cybersecurity audit completed in Q3 2024.

RISKS & OPPORTUNITIES

Key ESG Risks:
1. Physical climate risk: Supply chain disruption risk in Southeast Asian operations 
   due to flooding and extreme weather events.
2. Regulatory risk: Increasing regulatory pressure on Scope 3 emissions disclosure 
   under new EU CSRD standards.
3. Data privacy: Compliance complexity across 14 jurisdictions with different privacy laws.

Key Opportunities:
1. Green product line expansion — market growing at 15% CAGR
2. Carbon credits from emission reductions
3. ESG-linked financing at preferential rates
